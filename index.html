About Me

I'm a passionate mobile app developer with expertise in building high-quality cross-platform and native Android applications. With a strong foundation in Flutter and Kotlin, I specialize in creating performant, visually appealing, and user-friendly mobile experiences.

My journey in mobile development started with Android using Java, but I quickly transitioned to Kotlin when it became the official language for Android. When Flutter emerged as a powerful cross-platform solution, I embraced it to build apps that work seamlessly across multiple platforms while maintaining native-like performance.

I have a keen eye for UI/UX design and believe that great mobile experiences come from the perfect balance of beautiful interfaces, intuitive interactions, and solid technical implementation. I'm constantly learning new technologies and staying updated with the latest trends in mobile development.
